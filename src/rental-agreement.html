Thank you for considering Freeny Enterprise for your car rental needs. We are open Monday to Friday 9am-5pm, we appreciate your interest in our services. Before proceeding, please review the following important information regarding our rental process and policies:

1. Payment and Deposit: As a standard practice, renters are required to pay the weekly rental fee along with a deposit equivalent to the amount of the weekly payment. We will need to keep a card on file and also require a copy of your driver’s license for our records. Prices are non negotiable.

2023 Kia Rio, 2024 and 2020 Corolla $300/week plus $300 Deposit 2013, 2014 and 2015 Toyota Prius $250/week plus $250 Deposit 2018 and 2019 Hyundai Tucson $250/week and $250 Deposit 2011 and 2012 Toyota Prius $200/week and $200 Deposit

2. Vehicle Inspection and Test Drive: Upon arrival, you will have the opportunity to inspect the vehicle and take it for a test drive. We want to ensure that you are satisfied with the car before finalizing the rental agreement.

3. Insurance Requirement: It is mandatory for renters to obtain full coverage insurance before renting our vehicles. Additionally, you must add us as lienholders on your insurance policy. Please note that we do not provide insurance, and our company policy mandates that renters maintain full coverage insurance throughout the rental period.

4. Rental Agreement: After obtaining insurance coverage, you will be asked to sign our rental agreement. This agreement outlines our responsibilities regarding internal maintenance and any issues with the vehicle. However, renters are responsible for any external damages, tickets, tolls, or cleanliness issues during the rental period.

5. Payment and Deposit Return: Payment, along with the deposit, will be collected upon signing the rental agreement. The deposit will be returned within 10 business days after the vehicle is returned, provided there are no outstanding issues such as damages, cleaning fees, or fuel charges.

6. Area Restrictions: Our rental cars should not leave the DC, Virginia, and Maryland area. Any deviation from this policy may result in additional charges, including a fee of $500 if the car leaves the designated area. If the vehicle stops outside the approved area, you will be responsible for any costs incurred to return the car to our location.
